The whole of time

The entire life
of the universe.

From the first instant to the last — the Big Bang, the first light, the age of stars, the present moment, and the long darkness that comes after everything.

10⁻⁴³ s

The Planck epoch

Time itself begins. The four forces are one, and physics as we know it cannot yet describe a thing.

10⁻³² s

Inflation

In a sliver of an instant, space erupts — doubling over and over, smoothing the cosmos and seeding every structure to come.

1 second

The particle soup

A blazing plasma of quarks and light. Matter and antimatter annihilate; a tiny surplus of matter survives — and that is everything.

380,000 yr

First light

The fog clears and light flies free for the first time — the afterglow we still see as the cosmic microwave background.

200 Myr

The cosmic dawn

Gravity gathers the dark into the first suns. Light returns to a universe that had gone black.

1–10 Gyr

The age of galaxies

Stars swarm into galaxies, galaxies into clusters and a vast cosmic web. Generation after generation of stars forge the elements.

9.2 Gyr

The Sun is born

From the ashes of dead stars, our Sun and its worlds condense.

13.8 Gyr

Now. You are here.

A brief, bright moment when the universe is full of stars, and something is here to notice.

+4 Gyr

Andromeda arrives

The Andromeda galaxy collides with ours, and the night sky burns with a second river of stars.

100+ Gyr

The long isolation

Dark energy drives the galaxies apart faster than light can bridge them. One by one they slip over the horizon, and the sky empties.

100 Tyr

The last star dies

The gas runs out. The final star flickers and fades. The age of starlight is over.

10²⁵ yr

The degenerate era

Only cold embers remain, and slowly even the atoms within them begin to decay.

10⁴⁰ yr

The black hole era

For an unimaginable span, black holes are all that is left — bleeding away through Hawking radiation.

10¹⁰⁰ yr

Heat death

Nothing remains that can change. Maximum entropy — an endless, uniform dark. Not a bang, but the slow, final quiet.
